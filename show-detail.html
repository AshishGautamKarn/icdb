About Chef's Table

Synopsis

"Chef's Table" goes inside the lives and kitchens of six of the world's most renowned international chefs. Each episode focuses on a single chef and their unique look at their lives, talents and passion from their piece of culinary heaven. The series provides an intimate, informative, and visually stunning exploration of the culinary world, highlighting personal stories, inspirations, and the evolution of each chef's craft.

Created by David Gelb (director of "Jiro Dreams of Sushi"), the series has expanded beyond its initial run with several spin-offs focusing on specific themes like Pastry, BBQ, and France.

Style & Format

Known for its high production values, cinematic visuals, and in-depth biographical storytelling. Each episode is a self-contained portrait of a chef, combining interviews, kitchen footage, and exploration of their environment and influences.
