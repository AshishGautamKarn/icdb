About Gordon Ramsay

Gordon Ramsay OBE is a world-renowned British chef, restaurateur, television personality, and writer. His restaurants have been awarded 17 Michelin stars in total and currently hold a total of seven.

Known for his bluntness, fiery temper, strict demeanor, and use of expletives, Ramsay rose to fame on the British television series Boiling Point in 1999. He has since become one of the best-known and most influential chefs globally through shows like Hell's Kitchen, Kitchen Nightmares, and MasterChef.

Beyond television, Ramsay owns and operates a global network of successful restaurants across the UK, US, France, Singapore, Dubai, and more.

Trivia

  • Originally aspired to be a professional footballer before an injury ended his career prospects.
  • Trained under culinary legends like Marco Pierre White and Joël Robuchon.
  • Is a black belt in Karate.
