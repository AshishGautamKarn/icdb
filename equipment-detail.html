Product Overview

Description

The KitchenAid Artisan Series Tilt-Head Stand Mixer is a staple in home kitchens worldwide. Renowned for its durable all-metal construction and powerful motor, it handles everything from delicate meringues to dense bread doughs with ease. The 5-quart stainless steel bowl offers ample capacity for most home baking tasks.

Its tilt-head design allows for easy access to the bowl and beater, while the 10-speed control provides precision for a wide range of mixing tasks. The power hub fits numerous optional attachments (sold separately), transforming the mixer into a pasta maker, meat grinder, ice cream maker, and more, making it a truly versatile kitchen centerpiece.

Who Is This Model For?

Excellent for avid home bakers, frequent cooks, and those looking for a long-lasting, powerful mixer capable of handling a variety of tasks. Suitable for medium to large batches. May be overkill for very occasional users or those with limited counter space (consider smaller models).

Common Uses, Care & Tips

Common Uses

  • Kneading bread dough, pizza dough, pasta dough
  • Mixing cake batters, cookie dough, frostings
  • Whipping cream, egg whites, meringues
  • Making mashed potatoes
  • Shredding cooked chicken or pork (using flat beater)
  • Utilizing various attachments for grinding, pasta making, spiralizing, etc.

Care & Maintenance

  • Bowl, flat beater, and dough hook are typically dishwasher-safe (check specific model instructions).
  • Wire whip and pouring shield are usually hand-wash only.
  • Wipe down the mixer body with a damp cloth. Do not immerse the motor housing in water.
  • Periodically check attachment tightness and ensure the beater-to-bowl clearance is correct (adjustable via a screw).
  • Store attachments in the bowl or a designated drawer.

Community Tips & Tricks
